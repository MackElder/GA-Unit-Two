Which two websites are you most inspired by?

The dig-deep Wordpress site at Dinosaur Dracula

Badass Digest's responsive design and headline-style top strip.



How do they exhibit effective use of space, size, or balance?

I'm impressed by the block creation of news items on Badass Digest, a sleek and simple design that draws readers into the big topics. Dinosaur Dracula is cluttered like a pop-culture basement, but you're still drawn to the center main content.



What makes them similar and different?

Both seem to be built upon Wordpress themes, working on text as the main thrust. However, there's a cleaner, nearly antiseptic, look to Badass, while Dinosaur makes busy look engaging rather than annoying.
